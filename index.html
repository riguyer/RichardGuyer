Richard A. Guyer, MD, PhD

I am a general surgery resident at Massachusetts General Hospital, as well as a PhD-trained cell and developmental biologist. I am interested in using single cell tools and bioinformatic analysis to understand differentiation and pathogenesis in neural-crest derived cells.

This website collects my academic work, publications, software tools, thoughts about research and science, some of my favorite food, wine, and cocktails, and my contact information.
